Bay Area Nix User Group!

We are enthusiasts, users, and contributors passionate about the Nix package manager, NixOS, and declarative system configuration.

We meet monthly in the Bay Area, California, USA. There are no prerequisites or skills needed to attend and have fun. Come install Nix, work on Zero Hydra failures, or share what you've been working on. Our meetings are unstructured and welcoming to all.
